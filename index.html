Our Story

Where Celebrations
Meet Comfort

Established in 2025 and nestled near the iconic River Bridge,River Moon Resort is a serene sanctuary designed for unforgettable family getaways, weekend escapes, and landmark celebrations.

Our philosophy balances the stillness of flowing river waters with sophisticated hospitality infrastructure. Whether you are seeking a quiet retreat from the fast-paced energy of Kalyan or planning a grand social gathering, we provide a spacious, peaceful, and family-friendly atmosphere that immediately puts you at ease.

As the closest resort destination to the legendary Mandir , we offer unmatched convenience for families, event organizers, and spiritual pilgrims. We take immense pride in delivering top-tier amenities, comfortable accommodations, and highly professional service that ensures every single visit feels like a luxury retreat.
